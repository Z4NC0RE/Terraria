Weapons:

In Terraria there are tools and weapons, the tools are mosty an axe and a pickaxe with hammers, hooks, chainsaws, drills, and other tools to get the job needed done.


Weapons in Terraria are swords, yoyos, spears, boomerangs, flails and more for melee weapons. for ranged weapons there are bows, repeaters, guns, launchers, consumables, and more. For magic there are wands, magic weapons, spell books and more. There are many more weapons in terraria than this but this is a few examples.

Armor:

There are many types of armor some with special effects like you can jump higher, fly, do more damage, and even not take fall damage.
